Who was Max Brooks?

Maximillian Michael Brooks[1][2] (born May 22, 1972)[1] is an American actor and author. He is the son of comedy filmmaker Mel Brooks and actress Anne Bancroft. Much of Brooks's writing focuses on zombie stories. He is a senior fellow at the Modern War Institute at West Point, New York. In 2006, Brooks wrote the follow-up World War Z: An Oral History of the Zombie War, a novel on the same subject, set ten years following a zombie apocalypse.
